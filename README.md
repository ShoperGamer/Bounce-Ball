### ЁЯСЛ About This Project

**Bounce Ball Adventure** is a reimagining of the classic arcade genre in its "Ultimate" version. It focuses on fluid gameplay, modern graphics powered by advanced VFX, and a difficulty curve that scales with the player's skill.

### ЁЯЪА Project Features

-   **Advanced Visual Effects (VFX)**: Particle systems for explosions, glow effects, and ripple animations.
    
-   **Combo System**: Multiply your bonus points by hitting the ball consecutively.
    
-   **Dynamic Difficulty**: Ball speed increases every 50 points earned.
    
-   **Lively Environment**: Animated gradient backgrounds with shooting and twinkling stars.
    
-   **Power-up Items**: Randomized item drops to enhance gameplay variety.
    

### ЁЯТО Power-up System

-   ЁЯТЪ **Extra Life**: Grants an additional life (max 5).
    
-   ЁЯТО **Slow Motion**: Slows down time for 5 seconds for precision control.
    
-   ЁЯТ░ **Double Points**: Earn 2x score for a duration of 10 seconds.
    
-   ЁЯФи **Big Paddle**: Increases the width of your paddle.
    
-   ЁЯЫбя╕П **Shield**: Prevents the ball from falling (active until hit).
